About IdiomKu

A fun way to explore the richness of Malay idioms.

Hi there! I’m Julian, the creator of IdiomKu. In a former life, I spent a few months studying Bahasa Malaysia and interning at Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka (DBP), Malaysia's Institute of Language and Literature. During that time, I became fascinated by the richness of idioms and expressions woven into everyday Malay conversations — phrases that don’t always translate directly but offer unique and refreshing insights into Malaysian culture.

Although there are many great Malay language resources out there, I wanted to build something specifically for idioms that would be fun and accessible. I also wanted to experiment by creating a website almost entirely with AI. Enter Idiomku - A simple website to learn Malay idioms on the go. IdiomKu is a collection of categorized idioms that you can browse and quiz yourself on in your free time. Think of it as a cultural deep dive, one idiom at a time.

Meet Sang, our mascot! Sang is a clever mousedeer, inspired by the cleverness of people who masterfully use idioms in their daily lives. If I find the time, I’ll create more projects, like a guide to mastering Bahasa’s tricky imbuhan grammar forms. Stay tuned!
